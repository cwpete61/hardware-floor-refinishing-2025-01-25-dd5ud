Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the refinishing process take?

A typical refinishing project takes 3-5 days to complete. This includes 1 day for sanding, 1 day for staining (if requested), and 1-3 days for finishing coats and curing time. Larger projects may require additional time.

What types of finishes do you offer?

We offer water-based polyurethane (low-VOC, quick-drying), oil-based polyurethane (more durable), and natural oil finishes (penetrating). Each has unique benefits, and we'll help you choose the best option for your needs.

How should I prepare my home for refinishing?

Remove all furniture, window treatments, and wall decorations from the work area. We'll handle floor protection and dust containment. We recommend being off-site during the process due to odors from finishing products.

What maintenance is required after refinishing?

Regular sweeping and dust mopping, immediate cleanup of spills, and use of manufacturer-recommended cleaning products. We provide a detailed care guide and recommend professional cleaning every 12-18 months.

Do you offer a warranty on your work?

Yes, we provide a 5-year warranty on our craftsmanship and the finishing products we use. This covers any defects in the application process but excludes damage from water, pets, or improper maintenance.
